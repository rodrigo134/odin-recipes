Description

A classic homemade pizza recipe with a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings. Perfect for casual dinners and gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Optional toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 230°C (450°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to desired thickness.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Add your favorite toppings evenly over the cheese.
  6. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from oven, garnish with fresh basil leaves, slice, and serve.
