LASAGNA

Home

Description

A classic homemade lasagna recipe made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Perfect for family dinners and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • White sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a pan, cook the ground beef until browned. Add the tomato sauce and simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  3. In a separate pot, prepare the white sauce (béchamel) by mixing butter, flour, and milk until smooth and thickened.
  4. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Add a layer of lasagna noodles, then béchamel, then mozzarella. Repeat the layers until the ingredients are used up.
  5. Top with grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30–40 minutes, until golden and bubbly.
  7. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
